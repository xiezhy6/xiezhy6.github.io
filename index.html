Bio

I am currently a Ph.D. student in School of Intelligent Systems Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Prof. Xiaodan Liang. My research interests mainly lie in the Human-centric Synthesis, including but not limited to 2D/3D virtual try-on, 2D/3D-aware model/clothes synthesis/editing, cross-modal motion synthesis, cross-modal video synthesis, etc.

I recieved my B.S. and M.S. degrees from School of Computer Science and Engineering at Sun Yat-sen University, in 2018 and 2020, respectively.

I serve as an organizer for CVPR 2020 Workshop on Human-centric Image/Video Synthesis L.I.P. Besides, I also serve as a reviewer for CVPR, ECCV, ICCV, NeurIPS.
